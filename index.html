What is this game all about?

Guess Who is a game designed for children ages 4 to 6 to train various aspects of their spatial thinking and computational thinking skills.

How To Play

The goal of this game is for players to work collaboratively to decode the symbols and determine whose name is being represented.

1. Players will take turns choosing "name cards" to turn over.


2. Once the card is turned over, players will decode the symbols and numbers as they correspond to the alphabet grid.


3. As players follow the code on the name cards, they will arrive at different letters of the alphabet to reveal a name!


4. The educator will encourage students to verbalize the code using spatial language (i.e. 3 squares up, 2 squares right).
